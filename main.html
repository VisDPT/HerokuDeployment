ABOUT ME


I discovered my passion for UX/UI design when I was working as a traveling doctor of physical therapy! I lived in 5 cities over 6 years, which was how I entered the wonderful field of technology as a relocation blogger and soon to be developer.


Along the way, my passion for user experience design only grew and I just couldn't get enough of it! In my role at Oracle as a Cloud Solution Engineer with a UI/UX focus, I wore many different hats - User Experience Designer, Developer, and Client Facing Engineer. That's where my desire for becoming a UX designer was confirmed.
